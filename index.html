About John C. Maxwell

An internationally recognized leadership expert, speaker, coach, and author, John C. Maxwell has sold over 19 million books. Dr. Maxwell is the founder of EQUIP and the John Maxwell Company, organizations that have trained more than 5 million leaders worldwide. Every year he speaks to Fortune 500 companies, international government leaders, and organizations as diverse as the United States Military Academy at West Point, the National Football League, and the United Nations.

A New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and BusinessWeek bestselling author, Maxwell has written three books which have each sold more than one million copies: The 21 Irrefutable Laws of Leadership, Developing the Leader Within You, and The 21 Indispensable Qualities of a Leader.

Originally published by the American Management Association International (AMA), AMACOM became a sub-imprint of HarperCollins Leadership in 2018.

AMACOM is best known for its practical resources for specialized skills like HR, sales, project management, and many more, as well as general career and management advice to help you succeed at work and grow in your career.
